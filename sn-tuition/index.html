FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents ask before joining SN Tuitions.

Which classes do you teach? ▼
We teach students from Class 6 to Intermediate (Class 12). All subjects are covered with dedicated teachers for each subject.
Do you provide IIT JEE and NEET coaching? ▼
Yes! We have specialized faculty from IIT (MPC) who provide expert coaching for IIT JEE and NEET examinations.
Can we attend a free demo class? ▼
Absolutely! We offer a free demo class for all new students. Just WhatsApp us on call 9959256426 to book your free demo class today.
What are the class timings? ▼
Our classes run from 5:30 PM to 9:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. We are located at 2 Branches 1. Kottevari Veedhi, 8th Line, Ramavarapadu, 2. Main Road Kanuru Vijayawada.
Which boards do you cover? ▼
We cover CBSE, ICSE and SSC boards. Our faculty is well equipped to handle all board examination requirements and help students score maximum marks.
Is there CCTV monitoring? ▼
Yes! Complete CCTV surveillance is available throughout the premises for student safety. Parents can have complete peace of mind.
